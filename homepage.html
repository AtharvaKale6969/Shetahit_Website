Our Execution Chain

Farm Assessment

We assess quality of soil, climate condition, water availability, crop yield, livestock health, and infrastructure and management practices.

Reporting

Interpret on the analysis to identify strength, weakness and areas of improvement on the farm into the structured report with clear section for each aspect analyzed.

Service/Product Finalization

On the basis of gathered relevant information about the farm, we initiate with required service and products aligned under Shetahit.

On Ground Execution
